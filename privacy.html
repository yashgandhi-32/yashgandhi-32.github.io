Privacy Policy for Instant Meet App

Last updated: April 22, 2023

At [InstantMeet], we value our users' privacy and are committed to protecting their personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and store the data collected through our Slack app.

Data Collection

We only collect personal data that is necessary for the functionality of our Slack app. When a user installs our app, we ask for the below permission:

  • We take only command permission to add the "/meet" command to our Slack workspace.
  • We take calendar auth permission to create Google meeting links and add them to the user's calendar.

We only collect the user's email address as personal data. We use this data only to schedule meeting and add it to calendar

Data Storage

We take data security very seriously and store all data on a highly secure Digital Ocean server located in Frankfurt. We comply with data security measures with Digital Ocean

Data Deletion

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data at any time. If you would like to delete your data, please contact us at [support@instantapp.tech].

Third-party Access

We do not share or sell any personal data collected. We do use the services of the following external data processors:

  • Google Auth: To create Google meetings and add them to the user's calendar.
  • Lemonsqueezy (Payment Service): To accept contribution for the app.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this privacy policy at any time. We encourage our users to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at [support@instantapp.tech].
